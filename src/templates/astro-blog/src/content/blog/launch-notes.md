This starter keeps the official Astro blog template and layers in a few extra
defaults so content-heavy sites can start from a cleaner baseline.

## What is already here

- Official Astro blog structure
- RSS and sitemap support from the starter
- MDX-friendly authoring path
- Prettier for content and component formatting

## Suggested next steps

1. Replace the sample content with your own tone and categories.
2. Point `PUBLIC_SITE_URL` at the real production origin.
3. Decide whether the site is blog-first, docs-first, or marketing-first.
